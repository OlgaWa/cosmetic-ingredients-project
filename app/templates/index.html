Cosmetic Ingredients Finder

Search for cosmetic ingredients and find out their functions and descriptions!

Here you can find three search engines:

⭐ Cosmetic Ingredients Main Finder - four-step search engine where you can find out the exact names of cosmetic ingredients, their descriptions and functions.
⭐ INCI Functions Finder - search engine where you can find out more about functions of cosmetic ingredients.
⭐ INCI Abbrevations - search engine where you can find out what certain abbreviations of cosmetic ingredients mean.

* Database source: European Commission' Cosmetic ingredient database (CosIng) - Ingredients and Fragrance inventory:
https://ec.europa.eu/growth/tools-databases/cosing/index.cfm
