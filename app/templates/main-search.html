Cosmetic Ingredients Main Finder

Every cosmetic product in Europe must have a list of ingredients that it contains on its label. They have to be listed by their International Nomenclature of Cosmetic Ingredients names (INCI). Some examples of ingredients include oils, natural extracts, exfoliants or preservatives. If you don't know what some names mean, here you can find out!

One product can appear in many forms and therefore have many INCI names, e.g. shea butter - Butyrospermum Parkii Butter Extract, PEG-50 Shea Butter, Shea Butter Oleyl Esters.

Type the name of the ingredient in the field below and we will show you all the INCI names connected to it!
